AUTOMATING THE SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

  1. Home
  2. SCHOOL MANAGEMENT APP

Overview

This project was done at Teckplus Digital Solutions in Nigeria. Teckplus is a young and growing tech company in Lagos that is committed to bringing about transformation through innovations and inventions.

The services they offer include web development, mobile app development, cyber security, IOT and machine learning. The project is a comprehensive school management desktop application which is responsive and suitable for different screen sizes.


Identifying the problems

Initially, the first version of the app was designed to be used by any school in the world. However, after the deployment in a public school in Lagos, it was discovered that the app was too complex and had many unnecessary features which were not needed by the users.

This prompted the company to start another version of the app for only the schools in Nigeria.


Users and audience

Unlike the first version of the app which was designed to be used worldwide, the second version of this app was designed for the public and private schools in Nigeria.

My roles and responsibilities

  • wireframing
  • prototyping
  • Coordination of design team members
  • Collaboration with frontend and Backend team

Scope and constraints

In this project, I worked as the lead designer and coordinated my team to deliver a good product. My team encountered several challenges due to the fact that the previous administration did not properly arrange the existing design files.


The process

I started the design process by doing a user research. I conducted interviews with the users at the districts to know their needs, understand their behaviors and attitudes. I created a user persona for the project to guide the design process. I analyzed the data obtained from the research together with my team to identify user pain points and we deliberated on multiple ways of solving the problems. Through the use of scenarios, we were able to narrow down our proposed solutions . We created user personas and then use figma for the wireframing process. The design system of the company was used in creating the high-fidelity prototype for this project.

As as team, we designed the desktop, tablet and mobile views for all the portals. We had several meetings with the frontend and backend teams where we collaborated on the design and exchanged ideas.
