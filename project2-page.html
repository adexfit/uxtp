DESIGNING A CBT APP FOR SECONDARY SCHOOLS

  1. Home
  2. DESIGNING A CBT APP FOR SECONDARY SCHOOLS

Overview

This desktop app design was done for DevlanLabs. DevlanLabs is a tech company in Osun state Nigeria that is passionate about improving the quality of life of people through innovations that are capable of simplifying users' day-to-day activities.

The App was designed to familiarize senior secondary school students in Osun State with the use of the computer, computer-based tests and prepare them for the joint admission matriculation board examination which will help them to gain admission into the higher institution.




Identifying the problems

DevlanLabs conducted a quantitative study on the secondary schools in Osun State and discovered that most secondary students in the state don't get to familiarize themselves with computer-based tests till they write their first joint admission matriculation board examination (JAMB). Most of these students are only familiar with the paper and pencil test.

This always makes it difficult for them to effectively utilize the time given for the exam and answer more questions correctly.

Due to the huge number of registered students, most secondary schools spend a lot of money on printing exams and test question papers every year. Usually, this cost is passed down to the students which further increases their school fees.

Also, after spending much money on exams, some schools do have issues with missing exam papers while others are left with a huge number of scripts to mark manually. All these problems are what the desktop CBT app by DevlanLabs was designed to solve.


Users and audience

The CBT app was designed for Osun state public and private senior secondary school students and their teachers.

Challenges

As the only designer who worked on the project, I had to ensure that I delivered great work within a short period without altering the quality which required much dedication and hard work.


The process

Using the user-centered approach technique, I started the design process by doing a user research to understand the user behaviour and their pain points. I conducted interviews with the teachers and school principals of different schools in osun state and also observed the way their schools operate. Based on my observations and the analysis of the data obtained from the visit, I created user personas for the project and ideated on the possible means of solving the problem.

With the aid of scenarios, I was able to arrive at the best possible means of solving the problem. I created a design system for the project using Figma. The same software was used for wireframing. The low-fidelity prototype was tested using students and teachers from one of the school before it was developed into a high-fidelity mockup.

User testing

The low fidelity prototype was tested with two group of users; the students and the teachers. Most of the students navigated the app with ease. However, some of the teachers had problems with certain aspects of the admin dashboard. This was adjusted to ensure a better user experience on the desktop app.


Outcome and lesson

  • This design project taught me how to deliver great design within a short period without compromising quality.
